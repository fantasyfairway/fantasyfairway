How To Play


At the beginning of each week, users will be given a $1000 to spend on players to form their team.

Users will have the option to pick their team from over 100+ golfers on the PGA tour.

Golfers will be globally ranked and given a dollar value.

The top ten golfers will be valued at $500, golfers 11-20 will
be worth $200, and golfers 21+ will be valued at $100


Each user must spend their full $1000 dollars to form a team. Users can have as few as two top ranked golfers valued at $500 and as many as 10 golfers valued at $100

Each team must have their lineup set by the start of each tournament.

Scores for each team will be calculated relative to par. Meaning, if a team of two golfers shoot (-5) and (-10) the team score will be (-15).

Scores for rounds one through four will be added together to form a final score for each team
